## 5. WHY CALL OPTION IS UNFAVORABLE FOR SELLER

### Problems with EL's Call Option Proposal

| Issue | Impact | Risk to Seller |
|-------|--------|----------------|
| **Valuation Lock-In** | Prices fixed at 2025-2026 levels | If Mat Viet grows faster (↑ happened), seller misses upside |
| **No Flexibility** | Can't shop business to other buyers | Locked into EL, lose negotiation leverage |
| **2-Year Ambiguity** | EL decides IF/WHEN to exercise | Seller in limbo; no certainty on exit |
| **"Similar Standards"** | Undefined valuation methodology | EL can apply favorable assumptions (lower price) |
| **Downside Risk** | If Mat Viet stumbles, EL may not exercise | Seller bears downside, EL has no obligation |

### Why Full Acquisition > Call Option

✓ **Clear Price**: Negotiated upfront, no ambiguity
✓ **Certain Timing**: 12-month closing vs. 2-year wait
✓ **Leverage Preserved**: Can signal other buyers (PE, strategic)
✓ **Synergy Sharing**: Fair allocation between buyer & seller
✓ **Management Alignment**: Post-close earn-out incentivizes performance
